About This Report


This is a report by The Project on Accountable Justice, a think-tank based at the Florida State University College of Social Science Public Policy in partnership.

This report was written by Cyrus O'Brien, Researcher at the Project on Accountable Justice.

Research assistance was provided by Alexandra Raynolds and Nicholas Truitt, Interns at the Project on Accountable Justice and MA students at Florida State University College of Social Sciences and Public Policy.

Deborrah Brodsky, Director of the Project on Accountable Justice, edited this report.

This report was funded by the Charles Koch Foundation.
