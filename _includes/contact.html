Project on Accountable Justice

The Project on Accountable Justice (PAJ) is a think tank dedicated to advancing public safety through research and evidence.

PAJ is a collaboration of the Florida State University College of Social Sciences and Public Policy, the Institute for the Study of Prosocial Behavior at Baylor University, the Institute for Strategic Policy Solutions at St. Petersburg College and the Florida Public Safety Institute at Tallahassee Community College.
